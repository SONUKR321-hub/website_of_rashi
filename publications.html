Why We Chose This Topic?

We chose to explore dark matter because, as students of technology, we are deeply intrigued by the unknown and are driven by the desire to apply computational and analytical methods to unsolved problems in the universe. This interdisciplinary project provides a platform for us to bridge the gap between technology and space sciences, expanding our horizons in both fields while contributing to the ongoing quest for understanding the universe.
